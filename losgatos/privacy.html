Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Los Gatos Auto Service, Inc. ("we," "our," or "the Company") collects, uses, and protects information through the Los Gatos Middleware platform ("the Service").

1. Information We Collect

The Service connects to third-party business systems (including QuickBooks Online, TekMetric, and related platforms) on behalf of the Company to retrieve and process business data. This includes:

  • Financial records: accounts receivable, accounts payable, bank balances, profit and loss data
  • Customer and vendor names associated with invoices and bills
  • Payroll summary amounts and schedules
  • Vehicle and service records from shop management systems

The Service does not collect personal information from end consumers, website visitors, or the general public.

2. How We Use Information

All data retrieved by the Service is used exclusively for:

  • Populating cash flow forecasting models
  • Generating internal management reports
  • Operational analytics and business intelligence
  • Automating data transfer between authorized business systems

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on Company-controlled infrastructure
  • OAuth 2.0 tokens are stored securely and never exposed in logs or client-side code
  • No business data is sold, shared with, or disclosed to third parties
  • API communications use TLS encryption in transit

4. Third-Party Services

The Service authenticates with third-party platforms using OAuth 2.0. We access only the data scopes explicitly authorized by the account holder. We do not store third-party login credentials.

5. Data Retention

Business data is retained as long as it is operationally useful. The Company may delete or archive data at any time. Disconnecting the Service from a third-party platform revokes all API access.

6. Your Rights

The QuickBooks Online account holder may disconnect the Service at any time through QBO Settings, which immediately revokes API access. For questions about data handling, contact the Company directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the Service evolves. The effective date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
